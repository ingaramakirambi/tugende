📌 FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
❓ How do I change my travel date? You can modify your booking up to 2 hours before departure via "My Bookings" page or call our support line. Date change fee: 2,000 RWF if requested less than 4 hours before travel.
❓ What items are prohibited on the bus? Flammable materials, large animals (except service dogs), excessive alcohol, and sharp weapons. Small pets allowed in a carrier (extra 2,000 RWF).
❓ I lost my phone on the bus. What should I do? Contact our lost & found immediately at 📞 079 888 0000. We keep lost items for 14 days. Provide your bus number and travel date.
❓ Do I get a refund if the bus is delayed? If delay exceeds 60 minutes, you receive a 20% discount coupon for next trip. If cancelled due to weather, full refund or free rebooking.
❓ Can I buy ticket for someone else? Yes! Just fill their full name, ID number (optional), and contact info. The passenger must carry a printed or digital copy of the confirmation.
